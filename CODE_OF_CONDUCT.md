# Code of conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity or expression, experience, education,
socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion,
or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful and constructive; assume good intent while addressing concrete
impact; welcome questions; give and accept technical feedback graciously; and
put community and user safety ahead of personal advantage. Unacceptable
behavior includes harassment, threats, sexualized attention, discriminatory
language, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, sustained disruption, or other
conduct inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns through the contact information on the
[maintainer's GitHub profile](https://github.com/alireza-attari). Do not use the
public issue tracker for personal or sensitive details. Maintainers must protect
reporter privacy, recuse themselves from cases in which they are involved, and
apply proportionate corrective action. Actions may include a private warning,
temporary participation limits, removal of content, or a permanent ban.

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone officially represents
the project elsewhere. It is based on the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
